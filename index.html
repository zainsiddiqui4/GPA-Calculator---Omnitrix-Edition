Calculation Rules

Everything a first-semester student needs — formulas, steps, worked examples, and the exact logic this site runs on.

SGPA Calculation

SGPA (Semester GPA) measures your performance in one semester only.

Formula GPA = Σ (Grade Points × Credit Hours) ÷ Σ (Credit Hours)

Step-by-Step Method

  1. List all courses taken in the semester.
  2. Write the credit hours assigned to each course.
  3. Convert each letter grade into grade points (see Grading Scale below).
  4. Multiply grade points × credit hours = Quality Points (QP) for that course.
  5. Add the quality points from all courses.
  6. Divide total quality points by total credit hours.

Worked Example

CourseCHGradeGPQP = GP×CH
Programming Fundamentals4A4.0016.00
Calculus3B+3.339.99
English3A-3.6711.01
ICT Lab1B3.003.00
Total11——40.00
SGPA = 40.00 ÷ 11 = 3.64

Backend Logic (how this site computes it)

totalQP = 0; totalCH = 0;
for each course:
    gp = gradeMap[grade]        // e.g. "B+" → 3.33
    qp = gp * creditHours       // quality points
    totalQP += qp
    totalCH += creditHours
SGPA = totalQP / totalCH        // rounded to 2 decimals

Mathematically, this is a weighted average — courses with more credit hours pull your GPA more strongly toward their grade.

CGPA Calculation

CGPA (Cumulative GPA) measures your performance across all completed semesters.

Formula CGPA = Total Quality Points of All Semesters ÷ Total Credit Hours Completed

Step-by-Step Method

  1. Collect GPA and credit hours from each completed semester.
  2. Multiply each semester GPA × its credit hours = semester quality points.
  3. Add all semester quality points.
  4. Add all completed credit hours.
  5. Divide total quality points by total credit hours.

Worked Example

SemesterGPACHQP = GPA×CH
Semester 13.201754.40
Semester 23.601864.80
Semester 33.401654.40
Total—51173.60
CGPA = 173.60 ÷ 51 = 3.40

Backend Logic (how this site computes it)

totalQP = 0; totalCH = 0;
for each semester:
    qp = semesterGPA * creditHours
    totalQP += qp
    totalCH += creditHours
CGPA = totalQP / totalCH        // rounded to 2 decimals

SGPA vs CGPA — the difference

  • SGPA = snapshot of one semester.
  • CGPA = running average of your entire degree so far.
  • CGPA is a credit-hour-weighted average of your SGPAs — a heavy semester influences it more than a light one.
